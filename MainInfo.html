Why Use this Travel Site?

It's both a small world and big world out there.

This has one major goal: to give you unbiased, well researched travel information. The raw deal, with no overused cliches or marketing.

It is completely independently managed and researched. No promotions, no marketing, no overhyping or underhyping tourist destinations. The creator of this site understands that travel styles and activity preferences vary drastically different. This site therefore aims to give you the real deal, so you can figure out where you want to visit yourself.

The site also strongly emphasizes ethics in tourism, and how travelers can support the communites they visit.

Who should use this Travel Site?

While this travel site is primarily aimed at budget backpackers, the site's tips and destination reccomendations can be relevant to any type of traveler. The only types of travel which are for the most part, not discussed are cruise travel and amusement park travel.
